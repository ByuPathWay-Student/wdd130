Trips

Have the adventure you've dreamed of!

The Beginner's Adventure Trip takes you on a calm river to help you learn how to raft! This trip is fitting for people five years and older wanting to learn how to raft. This trip follows a straight and calmer river, it will help you ease into white water rafting.

The Rocky River Run Trip will give you a thrill while also not being too intense. It follows down a river surrounded by rocks, the river has some winding and more rapids than the beginner trips. This trip is built for slightly older people around twelve years and up.

Our Speeding River Trip is for people who want more intense rafting, with very choppy waters it'll give you the thrill you desire. It follows a speeding river downhill, it is the quickest of the two hard trips we offer because of how fast the water is. This trip requires that you are fifteen years or older because it is a more difficult trip.
