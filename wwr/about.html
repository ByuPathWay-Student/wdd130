Rapids White Water Rafting

We are dedicated to making white water rafting fun, affordable, safe, and memorable, so that you can have an adventure that you will remember for years!

History

Rapids White Water Rafting has been bringing the fun of white water rafting for 25 years! Rapids was created to help spread the joy of white water rafting. Rapids White Water Rafting was made for professionals and beginners alike!

The founder loved to white water raft, and wanted a way to share it with everyone. He created Rapids White Water Rafting to do just that. Rapids White Water Rafting has since then continued to make white water rafting safe and fun!
